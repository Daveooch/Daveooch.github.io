Watch_Dogs_2

You Can Pet The Dog!

From the infamous Ubisoft Games, Watch_Dogs_2 is a sequel to the beloved watchdogs series.
The story follows Marcus Hollow, a revolutionary hacker working with the notorious group Dedsec. The whole story follows Marcus and Deadsec trying to achieve the biggest hack in the history of the world by bringing down an invasive and corrupt system that's used to extort everyday civilians.

To answer the age old question: Yes, you can pet a dog in this video-game. The open-world is filled with a bunch of NPCs, some of which are dogs that can be pet! You can even get an achievement by petting the same dog 10 times, cool!
