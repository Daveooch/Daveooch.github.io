Red Dead Redemption 2

You Can Pet The Dog!

From the fantastic Rockstar Games, Red Dead Redemption 2 takes place in America, 1899.
The story follows Arthur Morgan and the notorious Van der Linde gang on the run after a robbery gone bad. To survive as outlaws in the last era of the wild-west, the Van der Linde gang is forced to fight for basic survival, be it because they need money, food, a place to stay, basic amenities, or even to rescue some of their members from the federals of America. You're questioned with morality through-out the story, as in-fighting begins and threatens the livelyhood of the gang. Arthur Morgan must make a difficult decision, does he abide by his morality, or break his moral values for the sake of the gang?

To answer the age old question: Yes, you can pet a dog in this video-game. The game takes place in an incredibly detailed open-world filled with towns, forests, camps, mountains, and early cities, allowing the player to freely explore and do what they please. Within the towns, you can come across domesticated dogs, which you can pet until your hearts content!
