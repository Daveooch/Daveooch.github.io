Fallout 4

You Can Pet The Dog!

Fallout 4 is a post-apocolyptic open-world RPG from the notorious Bethesda Games Studios.
The universe of Fallout is dark, gritty, unforgiving, and most definitely apocolyptic. The game takes place in the year 2287, two-hundred and ten years after a nuclear war broke out. Your time in the nuclear-wasteland can be spent by completing quests, leveling your character up, discovering locations, weapons, characters, and most imporantly, finding your kidnapped son!

To answer the age old question: Yes, you can pet a dog in this video-game. Early on in the game, you can come across a dog, which acts as a companion for the player. Amongst interacting with the dog, you are given four interaction options, one of which allows you to pet the dog!
