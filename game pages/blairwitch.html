Blair Witch

You Can Pet The Dog!

Based on the cult classic movies The Blair Witch Project, Blair Witch is a game that follows the story of a former police officer named Ellis. Ellis' motive is to find a boy that disapeared in the Black Hills Forest, with nobody but your pet dog and a flashlight. During the investigation, hell breaks loose, causing Ellis to get lost in a dangerous and haunted forest.

To answer the age old question: Yes, you can pet a dog in this video-game. Ellis has a dog-companion that follows him through-out the game, and actually impacts your story depending on what interactions you choose with him! One of the interactions the player can do is, you guessed it, pet the dog!
