Ark: Survival Evolved

You Can Pet The Dog?

From Studio Wildcard, Ark: Survival Evolved is a punishing jurrasic survival game, filled to the brim with prehistoric animals. You wake up stranded on an island with nothing to your name, other than one goal: survive. Ark allows players to craft tools, weapons, advance in technology (spear to gun), build, and tame the dinosaurs and creatures that fill the island!

To answer the age old question: Yes, you can kinda pet a dog in this video-game? As stated in the description, the game allows you to tame a plethora of prehistoric creatures, some of which can be pet by using the action key, although it's debatable if what you can pet is a 'dog'.
