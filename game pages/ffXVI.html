Final Fantasy XVI

You Can Pet The Dog!

Being the 16th installment of the Final Fantasy series, Final Fantasy XVI brings the player a dark-fantasy RPG where you follow the story of the warror Clive Rosfield. Sworn to protect his father's kingdom and to be the shield of his younger brother, Clive's story takes a nasty turn with the betrayel of his people and the corruption of the world he once knew.

To answer the age old question: Yes, you can pet a dog in this video-game. A little further in the prologue of the game, Clive gets ruinited with his pet-dog, Torgal! Interacting with Torgal makes Clive pet him.
